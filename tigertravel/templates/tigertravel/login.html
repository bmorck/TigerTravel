Step-by-Step Guide


Easy Login!

If you have not logged in before, use the "Continue With CAS" button to automatically create your profile with TigerTravel! If you have used TigerTravel before, you can simply click on "Find Me Some Travellers" and find your group!


Fill Out Your TigerTravel Form!

Finding a group is easy as ever. Fill out the form with a your Origin and Destination, your date of travel, and a departure timeframe that you are comfortable with, and we'll do the rest! You will instantly be notified by email about your group status!


Look at your Group Page!

On your group page you have all the information you need. Use our simple chat feature to communicate with your group members, and if you no longer want to be part of this trip, simply click "Leave Group" and your trip will be deleted!


Access your Profile Page!

You can view all of your rides on your private Profile Page! Your information has been collected from TigerBook, and you can click on any of your rides to access the group pages of those rides!
